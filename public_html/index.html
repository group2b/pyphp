Instructions for online admission

Welcome to the UMaT Online Application System.

  1. Step One: Upload your recent passport size picture.
  2. Step Two: Provide your Personal and Parental / Guardian Information.
  3. Step Three: Provide High School Records and the Grades Obtained.
  4. Step Four: Provide your program of choice and the source of funding.
  5. Step Five: Data Verification and Printing of Application Records.
